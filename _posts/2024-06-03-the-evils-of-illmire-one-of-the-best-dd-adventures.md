'The Evils of Ilm' offers more than just the standard module—it's a comprehensive toolkit including desktop backgrounds, GM screens, handouts, and even system conversions for D&D 5e, Sharp Swords & Sinister Spells, and Dungeon World. Whatever RPG system you prefer, 'The Evils of Ilm' is your versatile companion, ready to adapt with minimal setup.

Set in a village under the dark influence of a nightmare cult and an extra-dimensional entity known as the Fear Mother, this module weaves a complex web of intrigue and horror. As players navigate a community infiltrated by mind-controlling parasites, they encounter challenges that cater to all play styles—from combat to role-play to investigation.
