Discover how a simple act of reaching out to a role model turned into a career-defining moment, leading to collaborations on projects like Necromancer Games and his series, Points of Light. Rob discusses the challenges and triumphs of creating Black Marsh.

This setting has captivated players with its detailed and interactive environment, influencing how game masters and players approach their adventures.

From fan to creator, from initial sketches to a beloved game setting, Rob's story is a testament to creativity and dedication in tabletop RPGs.

Whether you're a long-time fan or new to the scene, this interview is packed with insights and inspiration.
