Join us in this enlightening group discussion as we explore the exciting new Kickstarter from Mary Mushman, featuring prominent authors Liam and Joseph. These creators have previously enchanted us with their unique and engaging adventures, and they're back to introduce their latest offerings through Kickstarter.

This video delves into the two new adventures being kickstarted and discusses the enhancements and expansions that breathe new life into these narratives. This session is not just a discussion; it's a celebration of creativity and evolution in RPG adventures.

Joseph and Liam share insights into their creative processes and the journey of their adventures from conception to the Kickstarter campaign. We also take a behind-the-scenes look at the role of Mary Mushman in bringing these projects to fruition, highlighting the synergy between authors and publishers in the RPG community.

Whether you're a long-time follower of Liam and Joseph's work or new to the RPG scene, this video will give you a closer look at the art of adventure crafting and the exciting potential of Kickstarter to bring creative visions to life.
